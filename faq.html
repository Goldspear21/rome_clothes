FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about ordering from ROME'S CLOTHES.

SHIPPING

Where do you ship?

We ship within the UAE. Delivery takes 3-5 business days.

How much does shipping cost?

Free shipping on all orders over AED 200. Orders under AED 200 have a flat rate of AED 25.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

Can I return my order?

Yes! We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

How do I return something?

Contact us at info@romesclothes.com with your order number. We'll send you return instructions and a prepaid shipping label.

SIZING

How do your sizes fit?

Our items run true to size. Check the product descriptions for specific measurements. When in doubt, size up for a more relaxed fit.

ORDERS & PAYMENT

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept orders through our website. Payment details will be provided via email after you place your order.

Can I cancel my order?

Contact us immediately at info@romesclothes.com. If your order hasn't shipped yet, we can cancel it.

CARE INSTRUCTIONS

How do I care for my ROME'S items?

Machine wash cold, inside out. Tumble dry low or hang dry. Do not bleach. Iron on low heat if needed.
